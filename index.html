About Me

My name is Mariner Folk. I have a degree in Psychology from Brigham Young University (Provo, Utah 2008). I love to study math and science; religion and mythology; and spirituality and intuition. Counseling is what I feel called to do. Love like a River is my own counseling method.

What is Love like a River?

Authenticity is living your life in accordance with your true self. Love like a River provides understanding of the different parts of the self that are often in conflict, and gives direction on harmonizing the parts for inner strength. Principles such as patience, humility, and diligence are drawn from ancient wisdom. Modern science and personal intuition add tools and insight towards healing and happiness.
